# Input Guidelines

## Allowed inputs (examples)
- Instagram username (for your own self-audit)
- Public profile fields you voluntarily share (bio text, links) 
- Your own notes about what you see on your profile
- Screenshots you took yourself, with personal identifiers/redactions applied

## Disallowed inputs
- Passwords, backup codes, cookies, session IDs, access tokens
- Private messages, private account data, or data you obtained without permission
- Doxxing content (full legal name, phone number, home address) unless it is removed/redacted

## Data handling rules
- Keep everything private: don’t upload sensitive details to public issues
- Use redaction-by-default
- Delete sensitive files after analysis if possible
