# Output Guidelines

## What the output should do
- Provide a clear risk overview
- Suggest concrete account hardening steps
- Use a friendly, non-judgmental tone
- Avoid guessing personal facts you haven’t verified

## What the output must avoid
- Instructions for hacking
- Collection of credentials or secrets
- Overly specific claims about identity unless supported by your provided input

## Privacy rules
- Encourage redaction of personal data
- Keep examples generic
