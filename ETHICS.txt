# Ethics Guidance (DataOfInsta)

## Core principle
Only perform security review on content you own or have explicit permission to assess.

## Prohibited behavior
- No unauthorized access attempts.
- No credential collection.
- No scraping or data harvesting that violates Instagram terms or user privacy.
- No publishing personal data of individuals.

## Safe alternatives
- Use your own public profile details as input.
- Redact personal identifiers from screenshots and reports.
- Keep analysis offline and store minimal data.

## When in doubt
Be conservative: if an action could enable misuse, it is out of scope.
