# Instagram Account Risks Checklist (Self-Audit)

## Identity & privacy exposure
- [ ] Bio contains phone number, full address, personal email, or unique identifiers
- [ ] Public links reveal other identities/accounts
- [ ] Location sharing is enabled too broadly (stories/posts)
- [ ] Highlights/recent posts reveal consistent routines (home/work)

## Account security controls
- [ ] Two-factor authentication (2FA) is enabled
- [ ] 2FA method is strong (authenticator app/security key > SMS)
- [ ] Password is unique and strong (not reused)
- [ ] Email recovery account is secured (strong password + 2FA)
- [ ] Login alerts are enabled

## Session & app hygiene
- [ ] Active sessions/devices reviewed and suspicious ones removed
- [ ] Old connected apps removed
- [ ] No unknown apps with account permissions

## Social engineering defenses
- [ ] Suspicious DMs are treated as scams until verified
- [ ] You don’t click “security verification” links
- [ ] You can recognize impersonation attempts

## Reporting readiness
- [ ] You have a plan for impersonation (preserve evidence, report fast)
- [ ] Friends/contacts know not to trust urgent requests from your account
