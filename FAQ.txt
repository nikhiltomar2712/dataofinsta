# FAQ - DataOfInsta

## What is DataOfInsta?
A documentation-first repo that helps you review what your Instagram presence might reveal and suggests security/privacy steps.

## Do I have to type my password or Instagram login?
No. Do not share passwords, cookies, tokens, or screenshots that contain secrets.

## Can this hack my Instagram account or someone else’s?
No. The project is not a hacking tool and does not attempt unauthorized access.

## What does “analyze your data like a fake account” mean?
It means the report is written from an attacker/impersonator perspective (privacy/threat modeling) to identify what could be inferred—not to actually exploit anything.

## What can I safely provide as input?
- An Instagram username
- Your own public profile text (bio, links) or screenshots that exclude private info
- Notes about what you observe (e.g., “I have a phone number shown”)

## Is the repository secure?
The repo itself does not provide guarantees about Instagram. Security assurance depends on how you use it and on following the repo’s no-credential/no-scraping rules.

## What if I find a security vulnerability in this repo?
Follow the responsible disclosure guidance in `SECURITY.md`.
